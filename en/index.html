Naka-mura Jukebox (formerly: Radio New Japan Broadcasting Club) is a dojin club in Chikuma city, Nagano pref.
Our main activities are with music label "NJB Records" and hentai game label "Ruritania".
It is our wish to contribute to the revitalisation of local communities through these activities.
